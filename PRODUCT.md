# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Manufacturing partners, food and cosmetics production companies, purchasing managers, and R&D specialists in Vietnam looking for food/cosmetic ingredients, chemicals, and industrial raw materials.

## Product Purpose
The localization, redesign, and optimization of the Sophchem website for Sophpower Vietnam, converting the complex original portal into a clean, highly optimized B2B landing page, product catalog, and administration dashboard tailored for the Vietnamese market.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Minimalist, Technical, Solid (Brand Green `#106d38`, structured layout, focus on industrial specifications, `rounded-xl` corners).

## Anti-references
- No purple or violet colors (strictly banned per quality guidelines).
- No over-rounded corners (max card radius `12px`-`16px` / `rounded-xl`, no `32px+` corners).
- No soft, wide drop shadows on bordered cards (the "ghost-card" pattern).
- No generic SaaS layouts with gradient text, decorative side-stripes, or floating glassmorphic panels.
- No cluttered animations that distract from technical data.

## Design Principles
1. **Flat UI & Geometric Precision**: Clean containers, hard borders, and flat catalog tables that make technical parameters easy to read and scan.
2. **Technical Content Focus**: Prioritize clear, tabular specification layouts for raw ingredients, regulatory certificates, and packaging units.
3. **Brand Green Uniformity**: Focus colors around Sophpower's official Brand Green (`#106d38`) for buttons, active tabs, highlights, and contact forms.
4. **Localization & Typographic Clarity**: Guarantee perfect font rendering for Vietnamese characters using Roboto and clean fallbacks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥4.5:1 for body copy and placeholders.
- Semantic keyboard navigation for catalog tabs and forms.
- Support for prefers-reduced-motion.
